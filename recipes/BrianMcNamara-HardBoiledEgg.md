### Hard Boiled Egg Recipe - Brian McNamara

## Ingredients
1 Egg

## Instructions
1. Place the egg in a pot
2. Pour in cold water until the egg is just covered
3. Place on the hob on a high heat
4. When it starts to boil turn down the heat, cover the pot and leave for 5 minutes
5. Empty the hot water
6. Tap the bottom of the egg on the edge of the pot to break the shell (You may need to cool the egg first)
7. Peel the remainder of the shell off
